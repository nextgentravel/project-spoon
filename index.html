Project Spoon: Travelling without a GC booking tool



Who should use this

Use this page if you are a part of Code for Canada and NextGen Travel’s “No travel booking tool” pilot. Here are some resources and tips to help you book travel for work.

Paying for your travel

Use a travel card to book all your travel. Either your personal credit card, or your individual designated travel card, which has a limit of $5,000 CAD.


Booking accommodations

Use the city rate limits table to find the spending limits for accommodation at your destination. Book directly with the hotel or through the service of your choice.


Booking a rental car

Use the car search page to find rental car companies and daily rates. Book directly through the company's website or over the phone. Don't forget to give the government rate code!


Booking a flight

You must take economy class, and choose the cheapest fare whenever possible. Book with the service of your choice.


Booking a train

Book directly through VIA Rail for travel within Canada.

The standard for rail travel is the next highest class after the full economy class. You are allowed to book business class!

For an 11% discount, use the Government of Canada discount code when booking: 700804.
